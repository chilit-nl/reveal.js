### Commits * Use a concise headline; max 50 chars * Only commit one thing at a time * Use the body to * give more context to what the motivation behind the change was * what exactly was changed in the code *
### Linear history ![nonlinear-vs-linear](1-nonlinear-vs-linear.png)
### Why a linear history * A non-linear history is hard to follow * Ordering of features and bugfixes is hard to determine\ * Removing specific commits can become harder when there are also merge commits to consider
